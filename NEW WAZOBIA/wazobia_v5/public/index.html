The Problem
Nigeria's electoral history is marred by violence, fraud, and disputed outcomes. The 2023 elections saw widespread allegations of manipulation. 93 million registered voters deserve a system they can trust. The manual process costs ₦300B+ per cycle and still produces contested results that threaten democratic stability across Africa's largest economy.
Our Solution
Wazobia Elect AI delivers end-to-end digital electoral infrastructure: BVAS biometric accreditation, blockchain vote recording, AI fraud detection, real-time Socket.io results aggregation, ZK-proof receipts, and multilingual civic education — all in one sovereign Nigerian platform.
Full Stack Technology
🟢 Node.js + Express 🔵 MongoDB + Mongoose 🟠 Socket.io Real-time 🟡 JWT Auth + bcrypt ⛓️ Blockchain Ledger 🔴 AES-256-GCM 🔍 ZK-Proofs 🤖 AI Fraud Detection 📊 Admin Dashboard API 📦 Rate Limiting + Helmet
